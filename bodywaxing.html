About Body Waxing

Body waxing is where I take wax and remove unwanted hair that you do not want. This unwanted hair can be anywhere on the body for both male and female anatomy clients, aside from the head for male and female anatomy clients and the beard area for male anatomy clients. This is a great service for people who are prone to ingrowns, razor burn or want longer lasting reuslts with their hair removal.

What To Do Before Your Body Wax

  • Grow your hair out to at least 1/2" or the size of a grain of rice.
  • Exfoliate your body very well to get rid of dead skin and release trapped hair.
  • Moisturize your body daily.
  • Take an OTC pain management, like Ibuprofen or Tylenol, according to what your PCP will allow to help alleviate any discomfort.

What To Do After Your Body Wax

  • Wear lose clean clothes after your appointment.
  • No sweating, friction, swimming or touching the area for at least 24 hours to prevent bacteria.
  • Continue exfoliating and moisturizing the area after 48 hours from your wax.
  • Rebook in 4-6 weeks for best results!
