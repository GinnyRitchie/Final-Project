About Permanent Jewelry

Permanent Jewelry is where you will pick out a piece of jewelry that is either Sterling Silver, 14K Gold Filled or Enamel and choose if you want it as a bracelet, anklet or necklace. From there, I will take a jump ring and close the two ends of the jewelry togther and use a small welder with argon gas and weld the jump ring closed. From there, you have a peice of permanent jewelry.

What To Do Before Permanent Jewelry

  • There is no prep before PJ!

How To Take Care of Your Permanent Jewelry

  • Do not pull on your PJ and try to be deligent about not getting it snagged.
  • Clean with a cleaning cloth that is provided if your PJ starts to tarnish.
  • If you need a body scan, like an MRI, and are required to take off your PJ, break at the jump ring and save it for a re-weld.
  • PJ may be a permanent peice of jewelry, but please remember that it is not indestructable, it is still delicate.
