About Advanced Facials

Advanced facials are great for the health of your skin. These appointments can be Dermaplane Facials, Nanoneedling facials, Acne Facials, Anti-Aging Facials, Chemical Peels and the Korean Klore Fcaials. I use Face Reality, Circadia, Hale & Hush and StoryDerm Korea skincare in my treatment room. What these facials all generally include are a couple cleanses, exfoliation, facial massage, mask with a scalp or shoulder massage, serums, lip treatment, Celluma LED red or blue light therapy and SPF.

What To Do Before Your Facial

  • Discontinue any serums or face creams for 7 days that contains acids like; Vitamin A/Retinol, Vitamin C, Glycolic Acid, Mandelic Acid, Salicylic Acid, any creams or ointments prescribed by a doctor for your face. If you are on Accutane, please discontinue 6-12MO before treatments.
  • Please come into your facial treatment without any makeup on.
  • Disclose any allergies that you have internally or topically before your appoinemnt.
  • Disclose any new medications that you are on, your skin can be extra sensitive on new medications.

What To Do After Your Facial

  • Avoid touching your face after a facial, you have a lot of bacteria on your hands.
  • Avoid the sun and wear SPF, especially if you've gottena Chemical Peel, nanoneedling facial, Dermaplane Facial or are sensitive.
  • Continue your skincare routine with your cleanser, moisturizer and SPF immediately.
  • Continue your serums and exfoliants in 7 days after your facial.
  • Mild peeling and changes in your skin will appear after Chemical Peels, Nanoneedling and certain Korean Facials. This is a normal process taht your skin will go through to help purge and correct your skin.
  • Rebook in 4-6 weeks and purchase aftercare products for best results!
