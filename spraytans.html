About Spray Tans

Spray Tans are great for people who do not want to use a tanning bed or lay out in the sun and risk skin damage. Spray tans are where I take a solution from Sjolie Solutions and spray a color that suits your skin tones well to give you a nice tan. This can be for an event like a wedding, prom or just for everyday wear to give you that beautiful color of the sun without damaging your skin or risking skin cancer.
